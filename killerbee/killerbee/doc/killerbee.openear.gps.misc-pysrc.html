 33      # meridian of latitude is given by 
 34      # 
 35      # R' = a * (1 - e^2) / (1 - e^2 * (sin(lat))^2)^(3/2)

 37      # where a is the equatorial radius, 
 38      # b is the polar radius, and 
 39      # e is the eccentricity of the ellipsoid = sqrt(1 - b^2/a^2)
